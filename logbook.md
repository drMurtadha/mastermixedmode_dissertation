# Week-by-Week Supervision Logbook  
## Master of Computer Science (Mixed Mode)  
## Dissertation Supervision Checklist

This supervision logbook is designed for weekly monitoring of a mixed-mode Master dissertation from topic selection until final submission. It is aligned with the dissertation assessment dimensions reflected in the uploaded evaluation form, including title, abstract, problem framing, objectives, scope, literature review, methodology, findings, discussion, conclusion, writing quality, significance, and overall recommendation. The form also indicates that examiners consider originality, critical ability, evidence of new findings, quality of presentation, and publication-worthiness.  [oai_citation:0‡SKM_C650i26032611060.pdf](sediment://file_000000001808720b895bb6f0bc0818f9)

---

## How to Use This Logbook

For each week, record:

- **Planned task**
- **Evidence submitted**
- **Supervisor feedback**
- **Action required before next meeting**
- **Status**

Suggested status labels:

- `Not Started`
- `In Progress`
- `Completed`
- `Revise`
- `Delayed`

## Standing Warnings to the Student

<ul>
  <li>Do not let the topic become broader over time.</li>
  <li>Do not continue if the research gap is still unclear.</li>
  <li>Do not write objectives that cannot be evaluated.</li>
  <li>Do not describe literature without critique.</li>
  <li>Do not design methodology that cannot be justified.</li>
  <li>Do not present results without interpretation.</li>
  <li>Do not claim contribution without evidence.</li>
  <li>Do not leave writing to the final stage.</li>
  <li>Do not ignore repeated feedback.</li>
  <li>Do not allow misalignment across chapters.</li>
</ul>
